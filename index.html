Opening Prayer

In the name of the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. Amen.

O Holy Spirit, Divine Guide, I open my heart to Your wisdom. Reveal to me the Scripture You have chosen for my soul today. Illuminate my mind and heart as I receive Your Word.

Holy Trinity, one God, speak to me through Your sacred text. Lead me in Your ways and grant me understanding. I am Yours completely, Lord, now and forever.

Glory be to the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit, as it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.

About This Tool

✝

This tool randomly selects a chapter from the Catholic Bible, which includes the 73 books recognized in the Catholic canon - 46 books of the Old Testament and 27 books of the New Testament.

The Catholic Bible includes 7 deuterocanonical books not found in Protestant Bibles: Tobit, Judith, 1 and 2 Maccabees, Wisdom, Sirach (Ecclesiasticus), and Baruch.
